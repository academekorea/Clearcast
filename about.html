Know what you're actually listening to

Podlens is a media literacy platform that helps podcast listeners understand the political framing, factual quality, and missing perspectives in every episode they hear.

The problem we're solving

Podcasts have become one of the most influential media formats in the world — yet there's almost no transparency about what you're hearing. Hosts build deep parasocial trust with their audiences, then use that trust to advance particular worldviews, often without listeners realizing it.

We believe every listener deserves the tools to understand what they're actually hearing — not to stop listening, but to listen with clearer eyes.

How it works

When you submit a YouTube or podcast URL, we transcribe the audio using AssemblyAI and then use Claude (Anthropic's AI) to analyze the transcript for political framing, language patterns, factual claims, and missing perspectives.

The result is a bias breakdown showing the percentage of content that leans left, is balanced, or leans right — with specific quotes from the episode as evidence. You also get a plain-English summary you can listen to, and a list of specific things to watch out for.

No data is sold. No profiles are built. Your analysis history stays in your browser.
