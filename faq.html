Frequently Asked Questions

How does Podlens analyze podcasts?
We transcribe the episode audio using AI transcription, then analyze the full transcript for political framing, language patterns, missing perspectives, and host influence. All analysis is grounded in the actual words spoken in the episode — not assumptions about the show, host, or network. Every finding is tied to specific, quotable moments.
Is Podlens free to use?
Yes. The free plan includes 4 analyses per month, full access to our discovery platform, a personal bias fingerprint that builds over time, and a weekly digest. No credit card required. Paid plans start at $8/month for more analyses and advanced features like source citations, audio briefings, and deeper analysis.
Which podcasts does Podlens support?
Any podcast with publicly available audio or captions. We support shows from all major platforms — just paste a YouTube video URL, a Spotify episode link, an Apple Podcasts URL, or any direct RSS/MP3 link. We support hundreds of thousands of shows across all genres and languages.
How accurate is the bias detection?
Our analysis is based entirely on the transcript — specific words, framing choices, and structural patterns. We don't make assumptions about a show based on reputation or network affiliation. Every finding is grounded in evidence from that specific episode. No automated system is perfect, and bias is inherently complex and context-dependent. We recommend treating our analysis as one informed perspective, not a final verdict.
Does Podlens support languages other than English?
Yes. We fully support Korean podcasts at podlens.app/kr with a localized experience and Korean-specific political framework. More languages are in active development.
How do I cancel my subscription?
Go to Settings → Subscription → Manage Billing. Your access continues until the end of your current billing period — you won't be charged again. For help, email hello@podlens.app.
Is my listening data private?
Yes. We never sell your personal data. Your listening history, analysis results, and bias fingerprint are private to your account and are never shared with third parties for advertising purposes. See our Privacy Policy for full details.
How do I contact support?
Email us at hello@podlens.app. We respond within 24 hours, usually faster.
