Privacy Policy

Effective date: January 1, 2026 · hello@podlens.app

What we collect

When you use Podlens, we collect only what is necessary to provide the service:

  • URLs you submit — to fetch and transcribe the podcast or video.
  • Transcripts — temporarily cached for 7 days to avoid re-processing the same content. Transcripts are never sold or shared with third parties.
  • Account information (if you sign up) — your email address and a hashed password. We do not collect payment information directly; payments are handled by a third-party processor.
  • Usage logs — standard server logs (IP address, timestamp, response code) retained for 30 days for security and debugging purposes.

What we do not collect

  • We do not build behavioral profiles or track you across the web.
  • We do not sell data to advertisers or data brokers.
  • We do not use tracking pixels, fingerprinting, or third-party analytics that phone home to ad networks.
  • Your analysis history is stored in your browser's localStorage unless you are logged in, in which case it is associated with your account only.

Third-party services

To provide the service we use the following third-party APIs:

  • AssemblyAI — transcription. Audio files are processed and discarded; AssemblyAI's privacy policy governs their handling of transcript data.
  • Anthropic (Claude API) — transcript analysis. Transcripts are sent to Anthropic's API for analysis. Anthropic does not train on API data submitted by default. See Anthropic's usage policies for details.
  • YouTube Data API — to verify video metadata. No personal data is sent.

Cookies

We use a single session cookie if you are logged in to Podlens. We do not use third-party cookies or advertising cookies.

Data retention

  • Cached transcripts: 7 days
  • Analysis job records: 30 days
  • Account data: retained until account deletion
  • Server logs: 30 days

Your rights

You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by emailing hello@podlens.app. We will complete deletion within 14 business days.

Children

Podlens is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 13.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated by email to registered users and by updating the effective date above.
